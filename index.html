Conservation Science

Joe F. Langley

University of Cambridge  ·  Human–Wildlife Conflict  ·  Africa

I study how humans and wildlife coexist — and when they don't. My research spans human–elephant conflict, continental-scale conservation forecasting, and the ecology of conflict across animal taxa. I work across sub-Saharan Africa and beyond, combining field ecology with quantitative methods.
